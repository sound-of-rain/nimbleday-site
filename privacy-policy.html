Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

NimbleDay is designed to help you plan your day with clarity. This policy explains what information is handled by the app and how it is used.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data on our servers from the NimbleDay app.

  • Your tasks, goals, journal entries, and preferences are stored on your device.
  • If you enable iCloud/Apple services, Apple may process data according to Apple’s own policies.
  • In-app purchases are processed by Apple via StoreKit.

How Data Is Used

Data in NimbleDay is used to power core app features such as planning views, reminders, widgets, insights, and optional calendar integration.

Calendar and Notifications

If you enable calendar sync or reminders, NimbleDay requests permission to access Apple Calendar and Notifications so these features can work. You can disable these permissions anytime in iOS Settings.

Third-Party Services

NimbleDay does not use third-party advertising SDKs or third-party analytics SDKs to track you across apps and websites.

Data Deletion

All your data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all your data at any time by deleting the NimbleDay app. If you have iCloud enabled, you can also remove synced data through Settings > Apple ID > iCloud.

Children's Privacy

NimbleDay is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at breathandspace@gmail.com.
